What Is Predictive Analytics?

Predictive Analytics is a powerful method that utilizes current and historical data to make predictions about future trends and
events. By analyzing relationships among various factors, it assesses risk, assigns scores, and empowers organizations
to be proactive, forward-looking, and make informed decisions based on data.
